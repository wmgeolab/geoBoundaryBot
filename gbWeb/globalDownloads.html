Comprehensive Global Administrative Zones (CGAZ)

CGAZ is a set of global composites for administrative boundaries. There are two important distinctions between our global product and individual country downloads. First, extensive simplification is performed to ensure that file sizes are small enough to be used in most traditional desktop software. Second, disputed areas are removed and replaced with polygons following US Department of State definitions. Attribution is required for all uses of this dataset.
